Usage

In this tab, no evolutionary process is at work. You can observe your individuals under different conditions, but they will not undergo any changes over time.

Header

Tracks

The Tracks button allows you to choose a track from your personal list.

Toolbar

Selector

The selector allows you to choose between displaying the Experimental Settings tab and the Laboratory tab.

Time Controller

It displays the playback speed. The double arrows on either side allow you to increase or decrease this speed.
The Play/Pause button allows you to start or pause the race.
The Start Over button clears all data related to the current process. Some experimental settings are reset.

Settings

The Minimum and Maximum speed sliders allow you to modify the speed limits of the cars.

Add a Car

This button allows you to add a new vehicle to the race, choosing its driver from your saved individuals or a human driver.
A view then appears, showing the driver's neural network.
On the left, a button allows you to change the color of the vehicle.
Another button allows you to remove the vehicle from the race.
If you add a human driver, you will be able to drive the car with the arrows on your keyboard (feel free to slow down playback time to help you).

If you add or modify the settings of a driver, the experiment will automatically restart.

Laboratory

Reproduction:

Choose two individuals from your list, then click on the Reproduce button: a floating window will ask you to save the individual resulting from the reproduction.

Mutation:

Choose one individual from your list, then click on the Mutate button: a floating window will ask you to save the mutated individual. A slider allows you to adjust the mutation rate.

For more information on reproduction and mutations, refer to the part of the guide on Genetic Algorithm.
