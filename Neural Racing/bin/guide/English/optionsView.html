Usage

This tab allows you to adjust the main settings of the software.

Header

Idiom

This button allows you to change the language of the software. Choose from the list of available languages, and the interface will be automatically updated.

Main View

Fading

When this setting is enabled, cars eliminated during a generation slowly disappear with a fade effect. Otherwise, they remain in place, motionless.

Hitbox

When this setting is enabled, the Evolution and Experiments tabs display lines useful for the proper functioning of the software:


The evolution tab with hitbox enabled.

  • In red, the physical bodies of the cars and the track.
  • In yellow, the reward lines, whose role is described in the part of the guide about Cars and tracks.
  • In shades of gray, the lines representing the cars' cameras. The whiteness of a line is proportional to the value of the camera it represents. More information on cameras in the tab of this guide about Cars and tracks.

Voice

When this setting is enabled, a voice announces the title of the software at launch.

Music

When this setting is enabled, music is played during the use of the software.

Data Table

It displays all your tracks, or all your individuals, depending on your selection. You can switch between them using the buttons at the top of the list. After selecting an item from the list, you can rename or delete it using the buttons at the bottom of the list. Note that you cannot rename or delete the default track.
